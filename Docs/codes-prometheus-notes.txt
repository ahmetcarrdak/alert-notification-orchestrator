Prometheus tek bir servise bağlı değildir.
Birden fazla target scrape eder.

Örnek:

scrape_configs:
  - job_name: 'accounting'
    static_configs:
      - targets: ['accounting-service:5021']

  - job_name: 'payment'
    static_configs:
      - targets: ['payment-service:5030']

  - job_name: 'reporting'
    static_configs:
      - targets: ['reporting-service:5040']
	 

	 
	  
Her servis:

/metrics endpoint açmalı

Yani her .NET servisinde:

.AddPrometheusExporter();
app.MapPrometheusScrapingEndpoint();

olmalı.
